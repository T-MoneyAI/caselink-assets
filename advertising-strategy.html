The Brief

One-line strategy

For Specialists
More referred patients, less admin chaos. CaseLink surfaces referrals that would otherwise get lost and gives specialists real-time visibility into their pipeline.
For GPs
Send better referrals, build better specialist relationships. Free forever — no barrier to adoption, immediate value from day one.
The real competitor is the fax machine. Not a SaaS product — a behavior. Every ad and asset has one job: make switching feel easy and make staying on fax feel embarrassing.

Creative angles by funnel stage

Awareness — make the pain obvious

  • "Your referral is sitting in a fax queue."
  • "The average dental referral takes 3 days. Yours doesn't have to."
  • "And that's where the story ends. It shouldn't."
  • "Still faxing referrals in 2026?"

Consideration — show the product

  • Before/after: fax workflow vs. CaseLink in 60 seconds
  • "GP sends referral. Specialist confirms. Patient gets care. No fax."
  • Dashboard screenshot: 23 new referrals, 85% conversion rate
  • Testimonial: "We saw 14 more specialist patients last month"

Conversion — remove friction

  • "Free for GPs. Always."
  • "Live in 5 minutes. No IT required."
  • "Join the DMV's fastest-growing dental network."
